ACTION SPACE:::::::::

Constructing action masks:

For the choice of movement; first select action head MOVEMENT, then mask out all positions that are not reachable (done by two functions, one is giving all possible nodes for the movement of the unit, another one masks out the ones blocked), then do softmax
THE BIG IDEA: As often as possible, mask out invalid actions as to simplify the life of the agent (by adding -infty to the logits before softmax, the prob gets 0)
